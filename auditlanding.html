What You Should Know

What happens *right* after I pay? +

You'll be immediately redirected to our secure intake form. After you submit the form, you'll get a link to book your 30‑minute walkthrough call (3–5 days out). We start the review immediately after we receive your intake.

What's the difference between the Free, Lite, and Full audits? +

• Free Checkpoint: A 15-min call to give a *verbal diagnosis* of one problem.
• Audit Lite ($1,750): A 3-5 day review to deliver a *written scorecard* of your top 3 problems.
• Full Audit ($3,500): A 2-week sprint to deliver a *full implementation blueprint* for your entire system.

Pro-Tip: Can I credit this toward the Full Audit? +

Yes. 100%. This is our most popular path. If you purchase the $1,750 "Audit Lite" and decide within 30 days that you're ready for the "Full AI Readiness Audit," we will apply your $1,750 payment as a full credit toward the $3,500 price.

What exactly do I receive? +

A 1‑page scorecard (PDF) outlining your top 3 revenue leaks and 3 prioritized, actionable fixes. You also get a 30‑minute walkthrough call with our team to discuss the findings and your next steps.

Do I need to prepare anything? +

Just complete the short intake form after purchase. It will ask what tools you use and what you *think* the biggest problem is. If we need secure, read‑only access to any tools for review, we'll request it securely.
