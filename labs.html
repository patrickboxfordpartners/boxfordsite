Interview Authenticity Intelligence

AI that detects when candidates are reading from scripts instead of thinking on their feet.

The Problem

  • Candidates use ChatGPT to craft perfect answers
  • They read from scripts positioned off-camera
  • Traditional AI interviews can't detect rehearsed responses
  • Hiring managers waste time on polished frauds
  • Remote work makes interview authenticity harder to gauge

The Solution

  • Real-time behavioral analysis during video interviews
  • Detect gaze patterns indicating off-screen reading
  • Analyze speech cadence and authenticity markers
  • Identify rehearsed vs. spontaneous responses
  • Give hiring managers integrity insights

Our Approach: Ethical AI Deployment

Candidates deserve to know they're being recorded and analyzed. We're transparent about using AI without telegraphing every technique. This maintains assessment integrity while respecting candidate privacy.

Our consent language:

"We record video interviews as part of our hiring process. This helps us evaluate all candidates fairly and consistently. Do you wish to continue?"
